Who we are

KISA (KAIST International Students Association) is a student-run organization, the primary concern of which is the well-being of international students. Split into 5 divisions (as outlined and described below), KISA does everything under its competence and jurisdiction to improve the experience of international students in KAIST. Our activities include, but are not limited to: Collecting and (re-)directing students’ concerns and suggestions to the appropriate administrative offices. Identifying and exploring the problems faced by the international students in KAIST. Proposing ideas and solutions to the KAIST administrative offices in order to alleviate the problems faced by the international students’ community. Ensuring the implementation of feasible solutions to the problems faced by international students. Presenting and forwarding the interests of international students. Creating, organizing and hosting events for international students at KAIST. For the points listed above, establishing and maintaining connections with other student bodies when appropriate.
