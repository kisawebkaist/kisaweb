KISA (KAIST International Students Association) is a student-run organization, the primary concern of which is the well-being of international students. Split into 5 divisions (as outlined and described below), KISA does everything under its competence and jurisdiction to improve the experience of international students in KAIST. Our activities include, but are not limited to:
  • Collecting and (re-)directing students’ concerns and suggestions to the appropriate administrative offices.
  • Identifying and exploring the problems faced by the international students in KAIST.
  • Proposing ideas and solutions to the KAIST administrative offices in order to alleviate the problems faced by the international students’ community.
  • Ensuring the implementation of feasible solutions to the problems faced by international students.
  • Presenting and forwarding the interests of international students.
  • Creating, organizing and hosting events for international students at KAIST.
  • For the points listed above, establishing and maintaining connections with other student bodies when appropriate.

Divisions
Split into 5 divisions (as outlined and described below), KISA does everything under its competence and jurisdiction to improve the experience of international students in KAIST. Divisions:
  • Events
  • Promotions and Public Relations
  • Welfare
  • Web
  • Finance and Logistics
Events
The KISA Events division is responsible for coming up with the ideas for new events, organizing the events in advance and conducting the events on the decided dates. We work with a dedicated team of people to ensure a brighter campus life for international students. Our Roles(Summary):
  • Creating Event proposals in advance
  • Communicating ideas and the proposal of events to the Promotions and Finance Divisions
  • Collaborating with the promotions division to come up with successful ways of promoting events.
  • Collaborating with the finance division for the efficient use of budget
  • Helping KAIST ISSS manage KAIST events e.g. KAIST ONE
  • Receiving reviews from the Welfare division to improve on future events
Promotions and Public Relations
The Promotions and Public Relations Division is the face of KISA. We are focused on leaving good impressions on all the people we work with, whether it be the international community, the administration of KAIST, or fellow clubs and organizations.PPR is in charge of making posters and other promotional material, handling KISA's social media presence, contacting groups and organizations to work with, and communicating with the international community. We also handle one of the latest KISA projects: our YouTube platform. Our roles:
  • Collaborate with other clubs/management in organizing events/welfare.
  • Maintain social media presence through Instagram and Facebook
  • Reaching out to the international students through email
  • Promote KISA Voice to international students
  • Promote YouTube channel by uploading better videos and following other promotion strategies.
  • Develop inter-university relationships to increase event audience and for getting ideas regarding the welfare of international students
  • (Korean affairs team) To translate documents written in Korean
  • (Korean affairs team) To promote KISA to koreans
Welfare
Welfare division's main focus is identifying and solving communal problems faced by the international community, from academic to social life, to maintain the well-being of the international students. We collect suggestions and complaints from international students by using both online and offline platforms (KISA Voice). Apart from that, our division will be re-starting the past welfare program, meal scholarship. Our roles:
  • to maintain the well-being of international community
  • to identify the problems faced by students
  • to coordinate with relevant offices to resolve the issues
Web
The Web Division works on maintaining and improving the KISA website and related applications, if any. We work on making the website as independent yet customizable as possible in order to connect the different divisions of KISA as well as the KAIST student base. We also come up with new features that we and the rest of KISA feel are beneficial to the workings of KISA as a whole.
Finance and Logistics
KISA Finance and Logistics division is basically responsible for two types of work - making all the finance transactions needed for the organization and managing the flow of things between any divisions. We are working with other organizations for funding our budget, allocating our resources for KISA events and products, finding and buying things we need, delivering it to the destination and organizing the venues of events Our roles:
  • to collaborate with ISSS on KISA sponsorship
  • to collaborate with other divisions on planning any KISA expenses
  • to buy required stuff for KISA work including organization of events
  • to keep track of all the transactions KISA made
  • to write financial reports of KISA events
  • to book and prepare the venues for KISA events
  • to deliver required staff for the KISA events
